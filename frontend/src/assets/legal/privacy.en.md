# Privacy Policy

## Personal data

This website does not collect personal data through contact forms, newsletters or other direct acquisition tools.

The only data stored locally is the user's language preference, saved via a technical cookie (`lang`) and in localStorage. This data is not shared with third parties nor used for profiling purposes.

## Navigation data

Server logs may record technical information (IP address, browser, request date/time) necessary for the operation of the service. This data is not cross-referenced with other sources and is retained only for the time strictly necessary for technical management.

## Third-party services

The website does not integrate tracking tools, analytics or third-party pixels. No content is loaded from external platforms (iframes, embeds) that could set their own cookies.

## Rights of the data subject

In compliance with the GDPR (EU Regulation 2016/679), users may exercise their rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) by contacting the data controller at the details provided on the Legal Notice page.

## Security

We adopt technical and organisational security measures to protect data from unauthorised access, loss or destruction.

## Changes to this policy

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page with an indication of the update date.
