# Cookie Policy

## What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on the user's device when visiting a website. They are used to improve the browsing experience.

## How we use cookies

We use technical cookies necessary for the site to function and, if configured in the project, optional analytics tools to understand how users interact with the site.

### Technical cookies in use

- `lang`: stores the language selected by the user (`it` or `en`) so the preference is kept across subsequent page reloads.

## Cookie control

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

## Changes to this policy

Any changes to this Policy will be posted on this page with an indication of the update date.
